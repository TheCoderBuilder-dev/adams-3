Adams Arcade

Kenya's First Shopping Centre — A Nairobi Heritage Landmark

1952 Seven Decades of Legacy
Discover

A timeless gathering place where Nairobi's past meets its vibrant present

Named after its founders Adams and Archibald, this beloved shopping centre has witnessed the birth of a nation, the growth of a city, and the stories of countless families who call Kilimani home. Today, over 40 businesses continue the tradition of community and commerce that began over seven decades ago.
